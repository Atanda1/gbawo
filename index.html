How to access Gbawo?

Once we launch, you can access Gbawo by visiting our website to get the link. You can also join our waitlist to get early access.

What is Gbawo?

Gbawo is a payment system that allows users to send and receive money on WhatsApp. The team's goal is to reimagine the way people actively interact with money.

How safe is using Gbawo?

Gbawo is 100% safe. Our AI agent uses the maximum level of security that WhatsApp offers.

Can other messaging apps use Gbawo

Currently, we're working on just whatsapp.

Who Is Gbawo For?

Gbawo is for everyone who sends messages on WhatsApp.

How do transactions happen on whatsapp when I send money?

Gbawo settles transactions in the background using stablecoins, while the recipient receives fiat. It's a fiat<>fiat transaction.
